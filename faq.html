Privacy & data

No. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties for any commercial purpose. What happens on this platform stays between you and this platform.

Your progress is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. This means it is private to you, but it also means progress will not carry over if you switch to a different browser or device.

Use the Restart This Course button on the final module of any course to clear that course's progress. To clear all data, go to your browser settings and clear site data for this page. For any other requests, email enquiries@safespot.life.
