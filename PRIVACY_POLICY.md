# Privacy Policy - Safe Learning Spot Centre

## Your Data Is Yours

We do not sell your data to third parties. What you do here is yours. Your progress, your reflections, your results — none of it leaves this platform for commercial purposes.

---

## What Data We Collect

### Essential Data (Required)
- **Email address** - for account login only
- **Password** - hashed and encrypted, never stored in plain text
- **First & Last Name** - for personalization only
- **Course Progress** - which courses you've started/completed
- **Feedback** - only if you choose to submit it

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Browser history or tracking pixels
- ❌ Device information or IP logging
- ❌ Third-party cookies or analytics
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Biometric data
- ❌ Payment information (handled by Stripe only)

---

## How Your Data Is Protected

### Technical Safeguards
1. **Encryption in Transit**: All data travels over HTTPS (SSL/TLS)
2. **Encryption at Rest**: Sensitive data encrypted in database
3. **Password Security**: Bcrypt hashing with salt
4. **Database Access**: Restricted to authorized personnel only
5. **No Third-Party Tracking**: Zero analytics cookies

### Access Control
- Only you can access your account
- Only you can see your course progress
- Only you can see your reflection answers
- Admins cannot view your private reflections

---

✗ Sell your data to advertisers

✗ Use your progress for profiling
