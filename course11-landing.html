Speaking With the Living World

The ancient practice of communicating with trees and plants — its scientific basis, Hermetic foundations, and practical method.

The natural world has been communicating continuously — through chemistry, electrical signals, and fungal networks — for as long as it has existed. The question this course addresses is not whether trees and plants communicate. That is now established science. The question is whether human beings can learn to consciously receive that communication.

This course draws on the Hermetic tradition of Thoth, modern plant biology, and indigenous wisdom traditions across cultures to offer a practical, grounded framework for entering into genuine relationship with the living world.

Eight modules. Practical throughout. No prior background required.

What you will cover
  • 01
    Everything Is Alive
    Biogenic intelligence, indigenous wisdom, and the scientific evidence that the natural world is communicating continuously.
  • 02
    What Thoth Knew
    The Hermetic tradition, the Emerald Tablet, and the seven principles that govern natural correspondence.
  • 03
    The Language of Trees
    The Wood Wide Web, volatile chemical signalling, electrical patterns, and learned behaviour in plants.
  • 04
    Entering the Frequency
    Preparing the body, the breath as bridge, and the role of physical grounding contact.
  • 05
    The Practice of Asking
    How to address a tree, the quality of questions that receive response, and holding the silence.
  • 06
    Receiving the Response
    The forms responses take, distinguishing reception from projection, and integration of what arrives.
  • 07
    Working With Specific Trees
    Oak, willow, elder, yew, and birch — their traditional attributes and the questions each one addresses.
  • 08
    Building the Daily Practice
    Regularity over intensity, seasonal practice, and the restoration of living-world attunement.
Time commitment: Approximately 4–5 hours across 8 modules. Work at your own pace.
