What you can control

We have designed this platform to minimise data collection. Below is a summary of the data stored on your device and how to manage it.

Course progress (localStorage)

Your progress through courses is saved automatically in your browser's localStorage under keys beginning with safespot_. This data never leaves your device.

To clear your progress:

  • Use the Restart This Course button on the final module of any course (clears progress for that course only)
  • Or open your browser's developer tools → Application → Local Storage → delete entries starting with safespot_
  • Or clear all site data from your browser's privacy/settings menu

Theme preference

Your light/dark mode preference is stored in localStorage under the key safespot_theme. Clearing site data will reset this to the default light mode.

Tracking & advertising

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels. There is nothing to opt out of in that regard — it simply does not exist on this platform.

Contact & communications

If you have contacted us by email, you may request that we delete your correspondence at any time by writing to enquiries@safespot.life.

Questions

If you have any questions about how data is handled on this platform, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us at enquiries@safespot.life.
