Where are you
in your healing?
This assessment moves through all ten areas of the Safe Spot curriculum: energy and relationships, toxic relationship patterns, the sacred energy body, financial sovereignty, generational wounding, institutional conditioning, media and narrative literacy, body and food wisdom, the art of becoming, and the uncharted mind. It is not a quiz. It is a mirror — built to show you, with some precision, where the most alive and unresolved work in your life sits right now.

There are no right answers. There are no wrong answers. There are only honest ones. The more honestly you answer, the more useful the picture at the end will be. Every question asks you to locate yourself as you actually are — not as you wish you were, not as you believe you should be. The wish and the should are the noise. Your actual position is the signal. Even the parts of you that would prefer to skip a question are information.

At the end you will receive a map — ten areas, ordered by where the most active patterns live. Each will carry a description of what that level of activation typically looks like, suggested first steps, and guidance for where to begin and why. These results are private and are never transmitted or stored anywhere. They belong entirely to you.

Before you begin. This is a self-reflection tool, not a clinical assessment. It does not replace therapy, counselling, or medical support. Some of what it surfaces may be difficult. If what comes up is heavy — stop. Take a breath. A day. A week. Come back when you are ready. If any section raises something that feels too large to carry alone, please seek professional support alongside this work. Awareness is the first step. A trained practitioner can help you take the ones that follow.

Allow between one and two hours. There is no time limit and no deadline. You can work through it across multiple sessions — simply return to this page and continue from where you stopped. Answer what you can today. The questions will wait. Your healing is not on a schedule.
