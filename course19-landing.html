The Digital Runway

Use the years before 16 as a runway, not a waiting room — and walk onto social media already knowing how it works.

In 2026 the UK announced that under-16s will be blocked from social media from early 2027. The worry behind it is real — but a ban presses pause without teaching, leaving a ‘cliff edge’ where teens are kept out and then dropped into the deep end at 16 with no practice. This free course is the missing manual: it uses the under-16 years as active training, so the cliff edge becomes a runway.

Across six modules you will learn to see how the feed really works (and why it’s not neutral), recognise the persuasive-design hooks that make it hard to put down, tell real from fake in an age of deepfakes, and build a sense of self that a ‘like’ count can’t shake — finishing with your own personal plan for staying safe and in charge.

Six modules. Written for teens (13–15), with a ‘for the adult reading along’ thread in every module. A short reflective question on each page. UK English.

What you will cover
  • 01
    The Cliff Edge
    Why the under-16 ban leaves a gap, and how to turn the wait into a runway: the three durable skills that travel.
  • 02
    How the Feed Really Works
    Recommendation algorithms, why you’re the product not the customer, and how feeds quietly narrow your world.
  • 03
    The Hooks: Persuasive Design
    Slot-machine mechanics, notifications and FOMO, dark patterns — and how to take back control.
  • 04
    Spotting Fakes
    Checking before you believe or share, deepfakes and AI fakery, and the tactics built to play you.
  • 05
    Identity Beyond the Like
    The highlight-reel comparison trap, validation loops, and building a sense of self the feed can’t shake.
  • 06
    Your Runway Plan
    Your own rules, staying safe and getting help (with UK resources), and walking onto any platform standing tall.
Time commitment: About 2 hours across 6 modules. Work at your own pace — there is a short reflective question on every page, and a task you can do together with a parent or teacher.
Before you begin

This course is free. No payment required.

This course teaches digital literacy and wellbeing — understanding how social media works and how to stay clear-eyed and safe. It does not teach ways to get around the law or platform age limits.

If anything online is harming or worrying you, please reach out: in the UK, Childline is free and confidential on 0800 1111 (for anyone under 19), or talk to a trusted adult. You are never stuck with it alone.
