We do not sell your data to third parties. What you do here is yours. Your progress, your reflections, your results — none of it leaves this platform for commercial purposes.

Think of us as windscreen wipers. This is your drive — your healing, your direction, your destination. We are here to keep the glass clear so you can see the signs along the road, without judgement.

The fees from this platform go directly toward funding SAFE youth hubs. Once we reach the right level of funding, every course on this platform will be free. Your investment now makes that possible.
